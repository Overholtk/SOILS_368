## Lab Questions

1. Describe how you would change your print layouts to a portrait orientation.
   In the *Item Properties* tab for the blank map layout page, change the *Orientation* field from landscape to portrait.
3. All the data layers used in this lab have meters as the linear unit of measure. Describe how to create a scale bar in the print composer so that the units shown on the map are in miles.
   In the *Item Properties* tab of the scale bar settings, select *Units* and then *Miles*. The conversion will be handled by Q automatically.
5. If you wanted to find out the names for the Lakes in the Lakes layer, where would you look?
   In the layer's attributes table.
7. What is the quickest way to zoom to an entire single data layer?
   Right clicking the layer and clicking *Zoom to Layer*
